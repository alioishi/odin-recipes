Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Roasted Brussels sprouts taste best when the inside is soft and caramlized while the outside is crispy. The best way to achieve this is to place Brussels sprouts cut side down when baking.

Ingredients

2 pounds Brussels sprouts

2 tablespoons oil or fat, such as olive oil (not extra-virgin), avocado oil, or melted ghee

1 teaspoon kosher salt

Pepper to taste
